.. _load_wsel_from_hdf:

Load :abbr:`WSEL (water surface elevation)` from HDF
----------------------------------------------------
Menu: 2D Results

Reads :abbr:`WSEL (water surface elevation)` data from HEC-RAS HDF results and loads it to current QGIS project as temporary layer. 

Save it to suitable location. The points can be then used to :ref:`create_wsel_raster`.
